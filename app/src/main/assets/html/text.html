Get Set GO is a time-management application that will help you to get things done!

Based on The Pomodoro Technique©, it alternates working time with small breaks. This will let you concentrate more easily and boost your productivity. No more distractions, focus on what’s important.

Health Issue

When you working for long period, two things need to be done periodically.

  • Standing up
    Sitting can be lethal. People who sit 11 hours a day have 40% increased risk of death within three years compared to people who sit 4 hour or less.
    Luckily taking a simple 5 minute break to walk around will remove the negative effect of sitting completely.
  • Drinking water
    This will make you refresh while working.

Get Set GO will remind you about water and standing up routine with Pomodoro to keep you healthy and more focused.
